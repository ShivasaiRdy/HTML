RCB VS RR IPL 2021 :
Maxwell explodes as RCB win by 7 wickets

Royal Challengers Bangalore defeated the Rajasthan Royals by 7 wickets in the Indian Premier League match on Wednesday. Virat Kohli and Devdutt Padikkal gave RCB a very fast start which was built upon by Srikar Bharat and Glenn Maxwell for a solid partnership in the middle-order. After Bharat was gone, Maxwell exploded and finished off the run chase with 17 balls to spare.
